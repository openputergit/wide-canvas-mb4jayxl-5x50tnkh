Week 24: Key Developments

Your baby's face is almost fully formed. The eyes are complete, though the iris still lacks pigment. The ears are well-established on the sides of the head.

The lungs are developing "branches" of the respiratory tree and cells that produce surfactant, a substance that helps the air sacs inflate with air.

Focus on iron-rich foods to support your increased blood volume and prevent anemia:

Safe Exercise

Exercise Guidelines for Week 24

During the second trimester, you can continue most of your pre-pregnancy exercises with some modifications:

  • Aim for 150 minutes of moderate activity per week
  • Avoid exercises that require lying flat on your back
  • Stay hydrated and avoid overheating
  • Listen to your body and rest when needed
  • Stop if you experience dizziness, shortness of breath, or pain
